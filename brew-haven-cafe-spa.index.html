We partner directly with small farms across Coorg, Chikmagalur, and internationally from Ethiopia and Colombia, roasting every batch in-house to honour the farmer's craft.

Where It All Began

A Cup That Changed Everything

It started with a single cup of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe — bright, floral, unlike anything founder Kavya Nair had tasted before. That cup, sipped at a tiny roastery in Copenhagen during a solo trip in 2018, planted a seed.

A year later, Brew Haven Café opened its doors on Church Street, Bengaluru. The concept was simple: bring the world's finest beans to the neighbourhood, roast them with respect, and serve them in a space that feels like a warm hug.

Five years on, we've grown from a 10-seat corner spot to a beloved community hub — but the soul remains the same. Every bean still tells a story. Every cup still matters.


What We Stand For

Our Values

Three principles that guide every decision we make — from bean to cup to community.

🌱

Sustainability First

We source from farms practicing regenerative agriculture, use compostable packaging, and offset our roasting carbon footprint. Good coffee should also be good for the planet.

🤝

Farmer Partnerships

We pay above fair-trade prices because the people who grow our beans deserve more than a fair deal — they deserve a great one. We visit our farm partners personally each year.

🎨

Craft & Curiosity

Coffee is never finished learning. Our baristas train monthly, experiment seasonally, and are encouraged to push boundaries. Boredom is the enemy of a good cup.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2018

The Spark in Copenhagen

Kavya tastes a life-changing cup of Ethiopian pour-over. The idea for Brew Haven is born.

January 2019

Doors Open on Church Street

Brew Haven opens with 10 seats, a 2-group espresso machine, and a menu of 8 drinks. The queue starts on day one.

2020

Roasting In-House

We invest in a 12-kg roaster and begin sourcing directly from Coorg and Chikmagalur farms. Our house blend is born.

2022

Expansion & Recognition

Named "Best Indie Café in Bengaluru" by Times Food. We expand to 45 seats and launch our seasonal menu programme.

Today

A Community Institution

2,000+ regulars, 12 bean origins, 40+ menu items, and a team of 14 passionate people. The story keeps being written.
